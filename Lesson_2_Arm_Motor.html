⚙️ Understanding Arm Motor Orientation

🔧 Critical: The Motor is Mounted Sideways!

↺

COUNTER-CLOCKWISE = ARM UP

  • ✅ Positive degree values
  • ✅ Moving from 0° to 90°
  • ✅ Lifts the arm higher
↻

CLOCKWISE = ARM DOWN

  • ✅ Decreasing degree values
  • ✅ Moving from 90° to 0°
  • ✅ Lowers the arm

📐 Position Reference Guide

0°

Arm Down

→

45°

Arm Middle

→

90°

Arm Up

💡 Remember: Because the motor is sideways, think of it like a windshield wiper - counter-clockwise sweeps UP, clockwise sweeps DOWN!
